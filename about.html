Welcome!

I grew up in Homer, Alaska (aka the Halibut fishing capital of the world). My Alaskan upbringing taught me to appreciate the outdoors, and when I'm not coding I'm usually outside getting some fresh air through hiking, running, kayaking, biking, or fishing. Growing up in the outdoors also taught me to be resourceful, and ever since I was a toddler I have loved making things. When I took my first CS class in college, I fell in love with programming and the ability to create my own projects from scratch.


My academic CS background has given me a strong foundation in algorithms and theory, but my desire to expand my web development skills lead me to enroll in App Academy, a 12-week intensive web development course where where I learned Ruby on Rails and Backbone.js. The practical skills I acquired at App Academy, coupled with my strong algorithm background from school, has given me a solid foundation in computer science that I can apply to all my projects.


I am a hard worker and I love opportunities to be creative and challenge myself. I am incredibly curious and love to learn new things. I am very independent, but I also love meeting new people, working in teams, and spending time with others.


I have a passion for technology, entrepreneurship, and all things innovative.
